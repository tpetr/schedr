    If you're coming to UMass as a freshman or transfer student, you're probably wondering (or worrying) about signing up for classes. UMass's course registration system is called SPIRE -- If you ask anyone who has used it, they'll agree that it's a pain in the rear to use. BUT, to quote Douglas Adams:

    Don't Panic!

    A UMass engineering student got fed up with SPIRE and decided to write a tool to help students sign up for classes quickly and easily. And that's how Schedr was born.

    Schedr simplifies the course selection process by letting students visually plan their course schedule by dragging and dropping blocks on a weekly calendar. All the data in Schedr corresponds to the actual course data for UMass. Here's a little video of Schedr in action:

    Don't forget that Schedr is only a tool -- it won't automatically register you for your classes (yet)! Once you've picked your courses in Schedr, click on the Register button at the top of the page to login to SPIRE and sign up for the classes you picked:


    That's all there is to it!

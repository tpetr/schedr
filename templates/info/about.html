About Schedr...

Schedr is a web application designed to help students plan their course schedule. It provides a visual drag-and-drop interface, which turns what was once a frustrating process quick and painless.

Schedr was started in the Winter of 2006 and has gone through a number of iterations. It's currently written in Python using the fantastic Django framework.

Are you interested in having Schedr support your school? Drop us a line at tpetr@schedr.com
